# 📊 Excel: E-Commerce Sales Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

* **Goal:** To build an interactive Excel dashboard to analyze sales performance across product categories, months, and regions.  
The dashboard enables users to select a product category and instantly view month-wise and region-wise sales trends.
* **Dataset:** Analyzed a dataset containing **50,000+ sales records** and **20+ business attributes**, including sales, profit, quantity, customer, product, and regional information.


---

## 🛠️ Tools Used

* Microsoft Excel (Advanced)

### ⚡ Excel Features Used

- `SUMIFS`
- `Combo Box`
- `Column Charts`
- `Data Analysis ToolPak`

---

## ⚙️ How I Built the Project (Step-by-Step)

1. Analyzed and explored a dataset containing 51,000+ sales records and 20+ business attributes.

2. Created month-wise sales and profit summary tables to analyze performance trends throughout the year.

3. Built region-wise sales summaries to compare revenue contribution across different geographic regions .

4. Applied `SUMIFS` formula to create different calculations based on selected product categories.

5. Added a `Combo Box` control to allow users to switch between product categories interactively.

6. Created column charts to visualize monthly and regional sales performance.

7. Designed a single-page interactive dashboard by combining histograms, bar charts and filters.

---

## 💡 Key Insights (What I Found)

* **Insight 1:** Fashion category generated the highest sales revenue ( $11,46,920 ) among all product categories.

* **Insight 2:** The African region contributed the largest share of total sales across all product categories.

* **Insight 3:** Sales activity showed stronger performance during the Wedding Seasons, Summer Vacations and Major Festive months of the year compared to other months.  
For Example - March to May, June & July and October months recorded the highest monthly sales value.

* **Insight 4:** Most orders get shipped within 5 to 9 days ensuring smooth deliveries and less delays.
